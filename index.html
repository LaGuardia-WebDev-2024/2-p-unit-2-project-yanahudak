Aoshima Island

The Best Island in Japan

Aoshima Island is an island tourists visit
to meet the large cat population. This population
outnumbers the human population on the island.

How to Get There

Take a local or rapid train on the JR Nichinan
Line from Miyazaki Station to Aoshima Station.
Then the island is about a five minute walk from the station.
